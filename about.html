About US

Pearlyii_ is a handmade accessories brand established in 2022 by Ladia Maylani. Inspired by the shimmer of pearls and heartfelt memories with her late father, Pearlyii_ represents self-expression through colors, handcrafted joy, and emotional stories.

The meaning of our tagline “Shine Bond Colorful Moment” is from the sparkle of beads and the memorable moments between the owner and the customers.

Pearlyii_ was established in 2022 out of a genuine love for bead-based accessories (especially bracelets) and a heartfelt connection to ladia's late father, who was deeply involved in her early creative journey.

Though there was no special launching ceremony, the emotional value was deeply personal. As Pearlyii_ grew organically, it became clear that the brand had potential beyond just being a hobby project. With a rising number of loyal customers, higher order volume, and the desire to stand out from competitors, a rebranding was necessary. Not to change the heart of the brand, but to clarify its story, solidify its personality, and elevate its presentation, both locally and globally.

VISION

To become a beloved handcrafted accessories brand that delivers not just beauty, but heartfelt connections through every bead and detail.

MISSION


1. To craft colorful, handmade accessories that let people express their personal style.
2. To design packaging that engages multiple senses-touch, scent and sound.
3. To make creative self-expression accessible through affordable, quality pieces.
