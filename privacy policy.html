Privacy Policy


What Information Do We Collect About You?

We collect the following information:

  • Registration information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Application usage activity (learning history, schedules, discussion forums)
  • Device information (device model, operating system)
  • Location data (if you enable location permissions)

How Do We Use Your Information?

We use the information we collect to:

  • Register you for pearlyii services
  • Provide a personalized shopping experience
  • Send notifications related to purchases and services
  • To develop and improve our products and website features
  • To provide user support services
  • To prevent misuse or security breaches

With Whom Do We Share Your Information?

We may share your information with:

  • Third-party service providers (such as cloud storage providers and notification services)
  • Legal authorities when required by applicable law
  • Marketing and business partners (with your consent) to improve services,
    offers, and shopping experience

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

How Do We Protect Your Information?

We take appropriate measures to protect your information, including:

  • Data encryption
  • Access protection through authentication
  • Monitoring of suspicious activity

We continuously update and improve our security systems to protect user data.

Cookies, Beacons, and Location Tracking

pearlyii uses cookies and other tracking technologies to:

  • Improve application performance
  • Personalize the user experience
  • Provide location-based services if you consent

You can reset or disable cookies and tracking through your device settings.
